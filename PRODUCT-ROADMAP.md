# Daily Habits — Product Roadmap

## Current State
Working prototype with: real-time sync, 70+ habit suggestions, customizable everything, streaks, points, confetti, 7PM reminders, parent dashboard, group scaling (7+ members), premium design with DM Serif Display + Sora typography.

---

## Phase 1: Use It (Now → 2-4 weeks)
Deploy for your family. Use daily. Keep notes on what's missing, what's annoying, what the kids ask for.

## Phase 2: Real App (Weeks 3-8)
- Firebase Authentication (login per person)
- PWA with app icon and splash screen
- Firebase Cloud Messaging (reliable push notifications)
- Editable task lists from within the app (done)
- Onboarding flow for new families (done)

## Phase 3: Beta (Weeks 8-14)
- 10-20 families from your network or ADHD parent groups
- Google Form for feedback
- Track: daily active users, day-7 retention, day-30 retention, completion rates

## Phase 4: Monetize (Weeks 14-20)
**Free:** Up to 6 members, default templates, 7-day history
**Family ($4.99/mo or $39.99/yr):** Full history, custom habits, push reminders, points, streaks, themes
**Group ($9.99/mo):** 7-25+ members, sorting, search, group stats, expandable cards

## Phase 5: Launch (Weeks 20-30)
1. TikTok/Reels — "mom with ADHD builds habit app so her daughters don't struggle like she did"
2. ADHD parent communities (Reddit, Facebook, ADDitude Magazine)
3. App Store / Google Play (wrap with Capacitor — $25 Google, $99/yr Apple)
4. Product Hunt
5. School parent networks

## Phase 6: Scale (6-12+ months)
- AI-powered task suggestions by age
- Teacher/therapist view
- Weekend vs weekday routines
- Habit insights ("Molly skips bathroom reset on Wednesdays")
- Family chat/kudos
- Multi-household support (co-parenting)

- Trademark search ($250-350)
