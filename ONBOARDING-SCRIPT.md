# Introducing Daily Habits to Your Family

## Frame: Independence, Not Punishment

**The why:**
> "I built something for our family. Instead of me constantly reminding everyone to do stuff, there's an app. You go through your day, check things off. I can see how everyone's doing, so I don't have to keep asking."

**The incentive:**
> "You earn points — 5 per habit, 20 bonus for finishing everything. 50 picks dinner, 100 gets extra screen time, 200 picks the movie. There's a streak counter too."

**Chores:**
> "Chores rotate automatically. The app decides whose turn it is."

**Solidarity:**
> "I'm using it too. Same habits, same streak. This is a family thing."

---

## First Week

- Check dashboard once in the evening, not constantly
- Say "nice" when you see 100% — keep it casual
- Don't punish incomplete days week one — the goal is opening the app
- Honor points immediately when redeemed
- After one week, ask what to add or remove

## Optional — The ADHD Angle

> "When I was your age, nobody taught me this stuff. My parents did everything for me, and figuring it all out as an adult was really hard. These aren't punishments — they're skills. And I'm practicing them too."
